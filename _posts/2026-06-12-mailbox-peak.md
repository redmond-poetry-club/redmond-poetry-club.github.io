title: "Mailbox Peak — A Student Poem"
date: 2026-06-12
---

Mailbox Peak,  
&emsp;a four-person fleet,  
On our feet,  
&emsp;what an amazing feat.

Old trail up,  
&emsp;on my quads.  
New trail down,  
&emsp;on my knees.

My camera celebrates,  
&emsp;yet my calf complains.  
The duck in my pocket,  
&emsp;and the poems remain.

*— a student*

> I took a rubber duck in the mailbox, and left in the mailbox my book of poetic lyric translations.
